FAQ - Have any Questions?

What is AlMulla Insurance Brokerage application?

It is an application designed to facilitate the process of search for the best insurance coverage in the Kuwaiti market.

How do you use the application?

You can download the app from Apple Store or Google Play or Play Store and register the data required, and then you can buy the needed type of insurance.

What can I do if I cannot register with the application?

Contact us at the call center and we will register you easily.

What kinds of insurance are there in the application?

All car insurance are available like, Comprehensive insurance and TPL insurance.Also others types of insurance like Liability insurance, Medical insurance, Fire insurance, Marine insurance etc.

What are the payment methods?

You can pay by K-Net card or by link payment.

How the insurance document will be delivered after issuance?

When payment is done, the document will sent to you within 24 hours according to your chosen address.

Can I request at any time?

Yes, but your request will be handled until our official working hours.

What happens if an error of order occurs?

Customer service staff will contact you to solve the issue and submit the right request, or you can directly contact our staff via live chat center 55901644.
