Motor Insurance

Motor Comprehensive
Insurance (MOD)

Comprehensive car insurance covers
damage caused by accidents that involve
colliding with another vehicle or object.

Motor Third Party Insurance
(M3P)

Motor Third Party Liability (MPL) covers
damage caused to someone else
vehicle.

General Insurance

Travel insurance

Travel insurance is coverage designed
to protect against risks and financial
losses that could happen while
traveling.

Medical insurance

Medical/Health insurance is a type of
insurance that covers medical expenses
that arise due to an illness. These
expenses could be related to
hospitalisation costs, cost of medicines
or doctor consultation fees.

Boat & Yacht Insurance

Boat & Yacht insurance provides indemnity
liability coverage for a sailing vessel
collision/TPL risks.

Cargo Insurance

Cargo insurance policies covers all
types of cargo transported by
various modes of conveyance such
as sea freight, airfreight and land
transit on an ‘all-risks’ basis.

Property Insurance

Property insurance covers
damage that results from Fire,
Theft, Wind-related events &
Acts of vandalism

Personal Accident Insurance

The Personal Accident Insurance
assures you against contingences
and it stands by you in difficult
periods by virtue of its coverage
and indemnity amounts.

Workmen Compensation Insurance Policy

A Workmen Compensation
Insurance Policy covers the legal
liability of an employer to provide
compensation to its workmen in
case of their death and accident, In
accordance with the provisions of
the Kuwaiti Labour Law.

Money Insurance

This policy provides coverage
against loss of cash or currency
whilst in transit as well as in your
premises due to accident or
misfortune.

Fidelity Guarantee
Insurance

A Fidelity Guarantee Insurance is
what your company needs to cover
a direct financial loss sustained by
an act of forgery and/ or fraud and/

Professional Indemnity Insurance

Professional Indemnity (PI) insurance
protects legal liability arising as a
result of breach of professional duty
due to any negligence act or omission
committed by the insured.

Contractors All Risk Policy (CAR)

physical loss or damage to construction
material, insured property, plant, machinery &
temporary structure like road, office,
shed etc. , at project site till the
completion of project.
